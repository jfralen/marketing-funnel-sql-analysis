# Key Insights

## Funnel Performance

* 54.9% of sessions progressed from view to click
* 80.1% of clicks reached add-to-cart
* Only 41.3% of add-to-cart sessions converted to purchase
* Largest drop-off occurs at the **checkout stage**

## Traffic Source Insights

* Email had the highest purchase efficiency (~19%)
* Organic drove the most traffic but had low conversion (~5%)
* Paid search balanced scale and conversion effectively

## Campaign Insights

* Paid Search (Cross-sell) generated the highest revenue (~$858K)
* Affiliate campaigns showed strong performance with lower refund rates
* Email campaigns performed consistently well across acquisition, retention, and reactivation

## Business Implications

* Optimize checkout experience to reduce drop-off
* Improve conversion of organic traffic
* Continue scaling high-performing Paid Search and Affiliate campaigns
